About

The South Eugene Robotics Team (SERT), founded in 2007, is the only FRC team in the Eugene/ Springfield area. SERT has had members from all around the Eugene/Springfield area, including home-schooled students and students from schools such as Willamette and North Eugene.

Most high-schools do not offer many hands-on mechanical classes, so SERT is one of the few opportunities for students to learn these skills which become incredibly benneficial throughout their lives.
